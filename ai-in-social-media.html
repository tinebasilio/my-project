AI in Social Media

Most of the top social networking companies have already adopted AI to scale up their processes and take their business to the next level. With artificial intelligence, social networking companies are analyzing voluminous data to find out what’s trending, different hashtags, and patterns. This analysis helps in understanding users’ behavior. Starting from notifications, to upgradations, everything is curated by AI. It considers all the past web searches, behaviors, interactions, and much more.

AI is a key component of the popular social networks you use every day. It can handle certain types of social media creation and management in minutes.

Here are some examples of how social media platforms are leveraging AI:

Facebook

The top social networking site uses powerful machine learning and AI to give users interest-based content, detect faces in images, suggest friends, recommend tag options , and target advertising to users. Facebook uses a variety of AI technologies to improve each user's experience.

Twitter

To create a thumbnail, the platform use AI to recognize a face in a full image. It uses neural networks to figure out which part of a picture the user wants. This technology is also used by Twitter to suggest replies when commenting on a tweet or responding to a comment.

Linkedin

This social networking site employs machine learning and artificial intelligence to predict suitable candidates for a certain job posting. Linkedin also use AI to identify applicants who are actively seeking new opportunities or who are most likely to reply.

Instagram

Instagram use artificial intelligence to recognize and recommend images and photographs. Instagram's first artificial intelligence application may be seen on its Explore page.

Youtube

According to TechRadar, Youtube is using artificial intelligence to block offensive content. During the ongoing pandemic, the world's largest video platform employed its AI skills to remove almost 11 million videos from its site.

Pinterest

The personalized content that Pinterest displays is one of the main reasons why many users love it. Instead of entering keywords, users may snap images using Pinterest Lens and use them to search for similar images. The platform use neural networking to offer users with content that is of interest to them. It means that images available on Pinterest are connected to a neural network based on a particular theme.
